Preeti & Gautam
A love story, 8 years in the making...

Preeti and Gautam met in September of 2003 while attending college in Bombay. Although two years apart, with seemingly not much in common, the two quickly became friends. Despite Gautam’s many attempts to teach Preeti how to code, their friendship survived and soon they couldn’t imagine a day go by without talking to one another. This bond only grew deeper as they both prepared to move to the US for further studies. Different country, different friends, different schools, and different time zones - not much could stand in the way of two people…who were still just friends.

Fast forward to 2011, Gautam finally put to words what they both felt in their hearts – that our lives would be incomplete without each other – and thus began the first chapter of their love story.
